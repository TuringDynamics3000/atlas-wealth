# External Data Provenance — Atlas Wealth

## Purpose
This notepad explains how external market and product data is sourced and used.

## Sources
External data may include:
- Index providers
- Product issuers
- Research providers (e.g. Morningstar)

## Controls
All external data:
- Is time-stamped at retrieval
- Is source-attributed
- Is hash-anchored
- Is snapshotted prior to use in advice

## Legal Intent
Advice reasoning relies only on information available at the time of advice.

This document is explanatory only.
