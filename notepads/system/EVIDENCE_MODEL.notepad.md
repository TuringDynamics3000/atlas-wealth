# Evidence Model — Atlas Wealth

## Definition
An Evidence Pack is an immutable collection of artefacts representing:
- what the system knew,
- what was proposed,
- what was approved.

## Contents
Evidence Packs may contain:
- Draft advice
- External data snapshots
- Policy constraints
- Authority signatures
- Linked notepads

## Immutability
Once created, Evidence Packs cannot be altered.

## Replay
Evidence Packs support deterministic replay for audit, dispute resolution,
and regulator review.
