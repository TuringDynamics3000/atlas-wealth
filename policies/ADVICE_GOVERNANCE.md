# Advice Governance Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines how advice is generated, reviewed, approved, and finalised
within Atlas Wealth.

## Core Rules
- Final advice must be approved by a licensed human adviser.
- AI systems may assist but may not approve or issue advice.
- All advice decisions must be evidence-backed and reproducible.

## Authority
This policy applies to all advice workflows and overrides any informal process.
